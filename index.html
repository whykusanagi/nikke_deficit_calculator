About the Formula

The stat penalty uses a piecewise function derived from the included dataset:

Stat Penalty = piecewise(deficit)
  • Low range (< 1%): Lookup table for accuracy
  • Main range (1% - 48%): Power law formula a × deficit^b
    • a = 1.039957
    • b = 0.478827
    • R² = 0.995062 (excellent fit)
  • High range (> 48%): Lookup table for accuracy

Dataset: Included in repository (self-contained)
501 data points covering 0.00% to 49.91% CP deficit

Tip: The penalty is active in Campaign (normal and hard), Tribe Towers, and Lost Sector. It is not applied in EX Battles, Simulation Room, Interception, or Coop.
